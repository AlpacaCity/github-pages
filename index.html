📃 Every tale has a beginning

Once upon a time, the founders of Alpaca City discovered this Alpacadia by chance when they voyaged through the Blockchain Ocean. They encountered for the first time, the most mysterious and beautiful crypto-species, Alpacacino. Those rare creatures are not only adorable on their own, but can also generate yields through their unique Alpa Energy. Now as the foundation of the city has been solidified on the Ethereum blockchain, Alpaca City decides to open its door to the outside world. 🌏 The city dedicates to provide fair distribution to its future residents regardless of the amount of assets they hold while hundreds and thousands of Alpacas await their future shepherds.

🏛️ Mission: Why are we here

Alpaca City is endeavoring to create a more accessible DeFi ecosystem by combining the power of yield farming and NFT. With low barrier of entry, the city aims to gamifying yield farming so the amount of asset is not the only determinant of the yield.

🦙 Overview: Welcome to Alpaca City

Alpaca is a rare and fascinating species with a wide variety of genetic traits 🧬. Their distinct traits not only make each Alpaca unique but also signify their different Energy levels.

  • Alpaca Core ERC-1155 contract address: 0xC7e5e9434f4a71e6dB978bD65B4D61D3593e5f27
  • You may adopt your first Alpaca by participating our NFT presale or buy one from Alpaca City Marketplace on Open Sea.
  • By letting your Alpacas manage your asset, you will be eligible to receive ALPA, the essential utility token of Alpaca City, as rewards.
  • ALPA will be distributed among all Alpacas based on the amount of assets they manage and their level of Energy. The more assets they manage, and the higher Energy they have, the more ALPA they will produce.
  • Alpaca's Energy is set at birth, but higher generations of Alpacas tend to have higher Energy. This might be why most Alpacas quite enjoy making babies. 😉
  • Breeding does come at a cost, but fortunately Alpaca City is redistributing the fees collected back to Alpaca Citizens through staking.
  • Currently there are 5,000 generation zero Alpacas in the city. Rumor has that there are a total of 50,000 generation zero Alpacas out there.

As citizens of Alpaca City, you will have the chance to own your one-of-a-kind Alpacacino and grow your own unique Alpa-herd.

🧬 Traits: The mysterious Alpaca gene

Alpacas‘ genes are fascinating. They determine the various traits Alpacas have and make every one of them unique.

  • Each Alpacacino has 12 different traits group.
  • 10 of the 12 traits are discovered and are directly linked to our appearances and energy level, while the other 2 remain unknown to us.
  • Among the 10 discoverable traits, each carries 4 genes - 1 dominant one and 3 secret ones. The dominant genes will determine the appearance and energy level.
  • However, the 3 secret genes could be passed on to the Alpaca babies when you breed them. So don't be surprised when you find the babies look different from their parents! 😉
  • It is discovered that in some very specific gene sequences, Alpaca's energy level gets enhanced significantly. This rarely happens and the true reason behind this is still left to be discovered. 🧐

⚡ Energy: Making your Alpaca powerful

Energy is very important.

  • The energy level is one of the factor that determines how much yield one Alpaca can generate when you let them manage your assets. Higher the energy level, the higher the yield.
  • Every single Alpaca was born with a different level of energy, which is influenced by genes and GN (generation number).
  • Each of these 10 traits, depending on the dominant gene, has an energy level of 1 / 5 / 10 or, in some rare cases, 15.
  • People learned that Alpacas Energy = sum(Traits Energy) * Generation Factor, and in most cases, higher generation leads to higher generation factor.
  • In some very rare cases, energy = 2 * sum(Traits Energy) * Generation Factor. This might has something to do with Alpaca's specific gene sequence, but the truth behind this is still left to be discovered.

👼 Breeding: Let's make babies

Breeding is the key to the long term prosperity of Alpaca City. It is no secret that baby Alpacas in the City usually have higher energy than their parents, and thus they have better capability in managing assets. However, as enticing as it is to make more babies, Alpacas do have some constraints:

  • ALPA (city's utility token) is needed for any two Alpacas to make babies. For each gen0 Alpaca, they need 10 ALPA to breed, this means the total breeding fee for two gen0 Alpacas will be 20 ALPA.
  • After breeding, Alpaca tends to get tired and the amount of ALPA needed will be doubled if you make them breed again (with a maximum of 16x base breeding fee). This increase in breeding fee resets after 6 hours.
  • As Alpacas' generations get higher, they need more ALPA to breed. (See breeding fee schedule below)
  • After breeding, it takes five minutes for the baby Alpaca to hatch.
  • After a baby Alpaca is born, it needs 24 hours before it can breed.
  • It is prohibited to breed with your parents and your children.
  • Cross generation breeding is allowed, but GN of the baby will be the minimum GN of either of the parents plus one. For instance, if an Almama has a GN of 6 and an Alpapa has a GN of 3, the baby alpa's GN will be 3+1=4.

💳 ALPA: The essential utility token

ALPA is the essential utility token in Alpaca City with the following functions:

  • Breeding: Power two Alpacas to make babies. ALPA spent in breeding fees will be collected by the protocol. The feature will be released right after the completion of ALPA Token Presale.
  • Staking: ALPA holders can stake in ALPA to become ALPA Stakeholder. ALPA Stakeholder earn 90% of the fees collected by the protocol. The remaining will go to Alpaca City team to ensure the sustainability of the project.
  • Voting: ALPA Stakeholder can vote on community proposals and decide the distribution of the community governance fund.
  • Spending: More features like Boutique will be released in the future to help gear up your Alpaca, increasing their energy while adding more utilities to ALPA. All fees collected to the protocol is subject to revenue sharing with ALPA Stakeholder as described above.

🌾 Farm: Yield farming with your Alpaca

Citizens of Alpaca are natural farmers. Unlike other places where farming yield depends on the amount of asset you own, farming yield in Alpaca City depends both on the amount of asset and the ability to farm (Alpaca's energy level).

🗝️ Staking: Earn as a Stakeholder

Unlike most cities where governments take high taxes, Alpaca City redistributes its income back to its citizens through staking. Breeding is currently the main use of ALPA token and there are more local constructions underway to boost the city's economy. Out of all the fees collected by Alpaca City, 90% is redistributed back to the city's stakeholders and the remaining 10% will be sent to the city's construction workers (dev team).

🛍️ Boutique: Gear up your Alpaca

Boutique is where you can purchase accessories to gear up your Alpaca and increase their energy. All purchases will be made in ALPA, collected by the protocol, and then redistribute between stakeholders. Boutique is currently under construction.
