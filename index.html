üìÉ Every tale has a beginning

Once upon a time, the founders of Alpaca City discovered this Alpacadia by chance when they voyaged through the Blockchain Ocean. They encountered for the first time, the most mysterious and beautiful crypto-species, Alpacacino. Those rare creatures are not only adorable on their own, but can also generate yields through their unique Alpa Energy. Now as the foundation of the city has been solidified on the Ethereum blockchain and Binance Smart Chain, Alpaca City decides to open its door to the outside world. üåè The city dedicates to provide fair distribution to its future residents regardless of the amount of assets they hold while hundreds and thousands of Alpacas await their future shepherds.

üèõÔ∏è Mission: Why are we here

Alpaca City is endeavoring to create a more accessible DeFi ecosystem by combining the power of yield farming and NFT. With low barrier of entry, the city aims to gamifying yield farming so the amount of asset is not the only determinant of the yield.

üß¨ Traits: The mysterious Alpaca gene

Alpacas‚Äò genes are fascinating. They determine the various traits Alpacas have and make every one of them unique.

  • Each Alpacacino has 12 different traits group.
  • 10 of the 12 traits are discovered and are directly linked to our appearances and energy level, while the other 2 remain unknown to us.
  • Among the 10 discoverable traits, each carries 4 genes - 1 dominant one and 3 secret ones. The dominant genes will determine the appearance and energy level.
  • However, the 3 secret genes could be passed on to the Alpaca babies when you breed them. So don't be surprised when you find the babies look different from their parents! üòâ
  • It is discovered that in some very specific gene sequences, Alpaca's energy level gets enhanced significantly. This rarely happens and the true reason behind this is still left to be discovered. üßê

‚ö° Energy: Making your Alpaca powerful

Energy is very important.

  • The energy level is one of the factor that determines how much yield one Alpaca can generate when you let them manage your assets in the Farm. Higher the energy level, the higher the yield.
  • Every single Alpaca was born with a different level of energy, which is influenced by genes and GN (generation number).
  • Each of these 10 traits, depending on the dominant gene, has an energy level of 1 / 5 / 10 or, in some rare cases, 15.
  • People learned that Alpacas Energy = sum(Traits Energy) * Generation Factor, and in most cases, higher generation leads to higher generation factor.
  • In some very rare cases, energy = 2 * sum(Traits Energy) * Generation Factor. This might has something to do with Alpaca's specific gene sequence, but the truth behind this is still left to be discovered.
  • More features related to the energy level of Alpacas may be released in the future.

üëº Breeding: Let's make babies

Breeding is the key to the long term prosperity of Alpaca City. It is no secret that baby Alpacas in the City usually have higher energy than their parents, and thus they have better capability in managing assets. However, as enticing as it is to make more babies, Alpacas do have some constraints:

  • ALPA (city's utility token) is needed for any two Alpacas to make babies. For each gen0 Alpaca, they need 10 ALPA to breed, this means the total breeding fee for two gen0 Alpacas will be 20 ALPA.
  • After breeding, Alpaca tends to get tired and the amount of ALPA needed will be doubled if you make them breed again (with a maximum of 8x base breeding fee). This increase in breeding fee resets after 7 days.
  • As Alpacas' generations get higher, they need more ALPA to breed. (See breeding fee schedule below)
  • After breeding, it takes five minutes for the baby Alpaca to hatch.
  • After a baby Alpaca is born, it needs 72 hours before it can breed.
  • It is prohibited to breed with your parents and your children.
  • Cross generation breeding is allowed, but GN of the baby will be the minimum GN of either of the parents plus one. For instance, if an Almama has a GN of 6 and an Alpapa has a GN of 3, the baby alpa's GN will be 3+1=4.

ü™ô ALPA: The essential utility token

ALPA is the essential utility token in Alpaca City. ALPA holders can use the token to breed Alpaca NFTs, voting for community governance, and spending in Alpaca City Ecosystem.

  • Breeding: Power two Alpacas to make babies. 90% of the ALPA spent in breeding fees will burned. Check burned ALPA tokens in this contract
  • Staking: Staking ALPA token to earn WOOL, which can be used to exchange Gen0 Alpaca NFT.
  • Voting: ALPA Stakeholder can vote on community proposals and decide the distribution of the community governance fund.
  • Spending: More features like Boutique will be released in the future to help gear up your Alpaca, increasing their energy while adding more utilities to ALPA. 90% of all fees collected to the protocol will be burned.

üöú Farm: Yield farming with your Alpaca

Citizens of Alpaca are natural ALPA farmers. There are two pools in Alpaca Farm: ALPA/ETH Uniswap LP pool and Alpaca NFT pool.

ALPA/ETH Uniswap LP Pool ‚Äî 70% of farming rewards (Only available on Ethereum)

In this pool, you stake in ALPA/ETH Uniswap LP token to farm ALPA. In addition to staking in LPs, you may choose ONE Alpaca (optional) to boost your farming yields. The more LP token you stake in, and the higher energy of the Alpaca Farmer, the more reward you will receive. Your pool share will be based on LP Amount * Alpaca Farmer Energy.

Alpaca Squad Pool ‚Äî 30% of farming rewards (50% on Ethereum & 50% on BSC)

In this pool, you need to assemble your Alpaca Squad to farm ALPA. The higher total energy of your Alpaca Squad, the more reward you will receive. Your share in the pool is determined by sum(squad energy) * avg(squad energy). For the opening week of Alpaca Squad pool, you can stake in at most 10 Alpaca NFTs to farm. This limit will increase by 5 every week, and the max limit is 30.

Start farming at https://alpaca.city/farm
